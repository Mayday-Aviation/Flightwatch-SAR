Operational Flow

From departure to shutdown
in four steps

FlightWatch mirrors how SAR flight watch actually works in a real Australian GA operation — without the whiteboards and phone chains.

1

Flight Lodged

Ops staff or the pilot enters the route, ETD, ETA, passengers, and SAR window. A unique pilot link is generated immediately.

2

Pilot Departs

Pilot confirms departure via their link. GPS tracking activates and the ETA countdown begins on the ops dashboard.

3

Live Tracking

Position breadcrumbs update throughout the flight. Ops can see the aircraft moving in real time without radio calls.

4

Arrival or Alert

Pilot confirms arrival to close the watch. If overdue, alerts fire automatically and the full SAR record is preserved.
