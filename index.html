Operational Flow

From departure to shutdown
in four steps

FlightWatch mirrors how SAR flight watch actually works in a real Australian GA operation — without the whiteboards and group chats.

1

Flight Lodged

Ops staff or the pilot enters the route, ETD, ETA, passengers, and SAR window. A flight manifest is generated and sent to the pilot immediately.

2

Pilot Departs

Pilot confirms departure via their flight manifest notification. GPS tracking activates and the ETA countdown begins on the ops dashboard.

3

Live Tracking

Position updates throughout the flight via phone GPS or paired device. Ops can see the aircraft progressing against the planned route in real time.

4

Arrival or Alert

Pilot confirms arrival to close the watch. If overdue, alerts fire automatically and the full SAR record is preserved for handover.
