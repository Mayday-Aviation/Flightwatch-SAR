SAR Escalation Checklist
Tap each step to mark complete. Follow in sequence.
1
Attempt radio contact
Call aircraft on last known frequency. Try 126.7 MHz (area) and 121.5 MHz (guard/emergency).
Immediately upon SARTIME expiry
2
Attempt phone contact — Pilot
Call pilot mobile. If no answer, try alternate contacts. Allow 5 minutes.
T+0 to T+5 min
3
Contact destination aerodrome
Call destination aerodrome or CTAF. Confirm whether aircraft has landed. Check for alternate diversions.
T+5 to T+10 min
4
Notify Ops Manager / Chief Pilot
Contact Ops Manager. Brief on aircraft, route, SARTIME, and all actions taken.
T+10 min
5
Contact Airservices Australia
Call 1300 306 630. Report: registration, pilot, route, SARTIME, souls on board, fuel endurance.
T+15 to T+30 min
6
Notify JRCC Australia if directed
Call 1800 815 257 only if directed. Provide full flight details. JRCC assumes SAR coordination.
As directed
7
Document all actions in SAR Log
Record every call, time, person, and outcome. Required for CASA and ATSB reporting.
